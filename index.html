About Michael

Building Kenya's Future Since 1990

Founded in 1990, Michael Owino Constructions has been serving Kenya for over 35 years with quality construction services. Michael Owino Omoro is a seasoned construction expert based in Kitengela, Kenya, with extensive experience in building residential homes, apartments, and large-scale commercial projects.

What sets Michael apart is his commitment to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and adherence to project timelines. His hands-on approach ensures that every project meets the highest standards of construction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our construction services

How long does a home construction project typically take?

The timeline depends on the size and complexity of the project. A standard 3-bedroom house typically takes 4-6 months, while larger projects like apartment buildings can take 8-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you help with building permits and approvals?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and approvals from local authorities including NCA, NEMA, and county government. Our team is experienced in navigating Kenya's construction regulations.

Do you provide free quotes and consultations?

Absolutely! We offer free initial consultations and detailed quotes with no obligation. Contact us to schedule a site visit and discuss your project requirements.

What areas in Kenya do you serve?

We primarily serve Nairobi, Kitengela, Athi River and surrounding areas, but have completed projects across Kenya including Kakamega, Mombasa, and Nakuru.

What is your payment structure?

We typically work with a milestone-based payment system: 20% deposit, 30% after foundation, 30% after roofing, and 20% upon completion. We're flexible and can customize payment plans based on project requirements.
