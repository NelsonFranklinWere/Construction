About Michael

30+ Years of Construction Excellence in Kitengela

Michael Owino Omoro is a seasoned construction expert based in Kitengela, Kenya, with over three decades of experience in building residential homes, apartments, and large-scale commercial projects.

What sets Michael apart is his commitment to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and adherence to project timelines. His hands-on approach ensures that every project meets the highest standards of construction.

  • Over 150 successful projects completed
  • Licensed and certified construction professional
  • Uses only high-quality materials from trusted suppliers
  • Committed to sustainable building practices
